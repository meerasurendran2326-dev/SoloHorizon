# Solo Horizons 🌍
### *A Safe Space for Solo Travelers*

> A web platform for solo travelers with AI-powered buddy matching, personalized travel planning, community forums and destination guides.

🏆 **3rd Prize (College Level) — TINK-HER-HACK-3**  
Kerala's Largest Women-Only Overnight Hackathon | Jan 25-26, 2025 | VAST, Thrissur

---

## ✨ Features
- AI Buddy Matching — connects travelers with similar interests and itineraries
- Personalized Travel Planning — custom trip recommendations
- Community Forums — share experiences and tips
- Destination Guides — curated guides for solo travel destinations
- Safe Travel Focus — built to empower women and solo travelers

## 🛠️ Tech Stack
`HTML` `CSS` `JavaScript`
